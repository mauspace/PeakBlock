# Privacy Policy for PeakBlock

**Last Updated: March 18, 2026**

PeakBlock ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the PeakBlock mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
PeakBlock is designed to operate with a "Privacy First" approach. 
- **User Data**: We do not collect any personal identification information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- **App Usage**: All configurations, blocks, and schedules are stored locally on your device and optionally synchronized to your private Firebase account if you choose to enable cloud sync.
- **Analytics**: If enabled, we may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., how many blocks are created) to improve the app experience.

## 2. How We Use Information
We use the information collected to:
- Provide and maintain the app's core functionality (blocking screens).
- Synchronize your data across your personal devices.
- Improve our services based on anonymous usage patterns.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage**: Your data is primarily stored on your device using encrypted local storage.
- **Cloud Sync**: If enabled, data is transmitted over secure SSL/TLS connections to Firebase (a Google service). We do not share this data with third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services
PeakBlock uses the following third-party services:
- **Firebase (Google)**: For optional cloud synchronization and anonymous analytics.
- **Expo**: For application infrastructure and updates.

## 5. Children's Privacy
PeakBlock does not knowingly collect any PII from children under the age of 13.

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time by clearing the app's cache or deleting your Firebase account.
